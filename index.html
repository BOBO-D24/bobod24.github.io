About Me

I’m Adeola Majiyagbe, mostly known as Bobo D a passionate Front-End Developer who turns ideas into clean, functional, and visually compelling digital experiences. With over five years of hands-on experience, I’ve worked across startups and enterprise environments, building scalable web applications that balance performance with elegant design. My background in computer science gives me a strong technical foundation, but what truly defines my work is my problem-solving mindset I enjoy breaking down complex challenges and transforming them into simple, intuitive solutions.

One of my most significant projects is XalesFlow, a retail and inventory management system I architected and built from the ground up. What started as an idea to simplify sales automation evolved into a production-ready desktop solution used in real-world retail and hospitality environments.

Outside of tech, I embrace creativity and competition. I’m an active gamer, especially into Call of Duty, where strategy, reflex, and tactical thinking come into play. Music is another space where I recharge whether discovering new sounds or revisiting timeless classics. And when it’s time to unwind, you’ll likely find me deep into sci-fi movies, fascinated by futuristic worlds, advanced civilizations, and the possibilities of tomorrow.
